Duo Mobile 4

Since 2020 I’ve been working on the redesign of the Duo Mobile app, a product that is used by about 12 million users as their second factor of authentication. Alongside the Duo Authentication Prompt, it’s the most visible touchpoint for Duo.

It was an exciting product to work on because I believe it helps people secure their logins easily by creating an intuitive frictionless expericence. The redesign for Duo Mobile app is a part of a bigger initiative of launching a modernized, accessible authentication experience. This redesign is the most significant mobile app update for Duo in 8 years and launched in the app store for general access October 2021.

My Role

I led the overall UX vision, design and implementation for the Duo Mobile Android app, working closely with our Android engineering team and collaborating closely with our product manager, iOS designer and user researcher. Here is our lovely mobile team:

The Goal

Our goal for the redesign was to make the Duo Mobile app easier for users to enroll and authenticate. In addition to that, we also wanted to enable users to be able to self-remediate in the app, rather than call the help desk. Accessibility was another thing we wanted to achieve, keeping all users in mind while designing the app.

Features

In the new design, we focused on improving the authentication experience for all use cases, educating first-time users, and empowering advanced users. Some of the core features I worked on were:

  • Accounts
  • Duo Push transaction experience
  • Enrollment experience
  • Android account restore experience
  • Security checkup experience
  • Dark theme

Know our users

To provide better experiences for our users, we want to understand their current workflows and pain points better. We worked with our user researcher to create research plans, including surveys and user interviews, to understand more about user behaviors. We gained great insights from the synthesis of these researches and incorporated them into our design iteration.

The style

Since Duo Mobile is part of the Duo 4 cross-platform authentication experience, keeping style consistent with other Duo products was central to my work. I leveraged our Duo Tellaro Design System for desktop and Android Material Design to create the Android style. It's clean, simple, and familiar to Android users.
