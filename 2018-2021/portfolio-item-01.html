PetBook

Neighborhood Pet Social Network

PROBLEM STATEMENT

While taking care of cat in my neighborhood, I noticed that there is no online platform for pet owners to share useful information about having a pet in the neighborhood and lots of pet owners need this information to solve the problems of their own pets.

SOLUTON

The PetBook app design is based on two main needs of pet lovers : sharing useful information and showing off pets. It has two main functions: the pet forum and pet timeline. The pet forum is for exchanging all kinds of information and the pet timeline is to record pet's life. launch prototype here

Signup & Pet Timeline

Singup for more than one pet
Record pet life using pet timeline
View other user’s pet timeline
Communicate: like and comment

Pet Forum

Create posts
View posts
Answering questions
Sort & search posts

Pet Chat & Neighborhood Map

Chat with other pet owners
View chat history
New chat notification
View other pet's location

Target Audience

Pet owners who own one or multiple pets in this neighborhood, pet lovers in this neighborhood who loves pet but don’t have one now and staff of nearby pet-related businesses

Personas & Scenarios

Experienced pet owners
New pet owners
staff of nearby pet-related businesses

COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS

During the competitive analysis research of these four websites and apps, based on the user quantity and post quality, I found out simpler and more organized website and app is more popular. To achieve the goal of simple and clear, sometimes you have to give up some not so highlighted functions.

STORYBOARD

After the interview with 5 local pet owners, I sketched out all the possible activities the pet owners want in this PetBook app and figured out how these activities connect with each other

WIREFRAMING

Low fidelity wireframe for PetBook mobile app

Paper Prototye

I created the paper Prototye of PetBook app first and did usability test with 3 pet owners using this paper prototype. After collecting feedback from them, I improved the prototype many times and then developed the digital prototype in Axure PR

Description

PetBook is an online pet social network app that provides convenience to pet owners and gives them opportunities to get to know each other better.

The critical feature of this application is pet information sharing. Pet owners, pet lovers and staff from pet-related business all give information they have and get information they need from this app. Neighbors help each other out and get to know each other better by sharing the useful information about how to take care of pets.
