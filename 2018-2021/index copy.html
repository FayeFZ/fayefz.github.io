Hi. I am Fei Zhao :)

I am a UX designer based in Ann Arbor, Michigan.

My passion for design and technology drove me to pursue a career as a UX designer after finishing my first master degree in Landscape Architecture. From landscape design to digital product design, although the scales are very different - miles (cites & parks), yards (gardens & monitors), feet (tablet), and inches (mobile phones & wearable devices) - the concept of designing for people has always been my passion and interest.
